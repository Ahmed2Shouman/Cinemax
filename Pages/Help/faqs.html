Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a movie ticket?

You can book tickets by selecting your movie from the homepage or movies section, choosing a showtime, and completing payment through our secure system.

Can I cancel or refund my booking?

Tickets can be canceled up to 2 hours before the showtime through your account under 'My Bookings'. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer group discounts for bookings of 10 or more tickets. Please contact our customer support for more information.
